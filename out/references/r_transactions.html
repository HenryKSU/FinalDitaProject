Transactions

Picture of Transaction screen

This is where you can track and organize all your Transactions. There are 3 different options to receive your transactions. You can use these 3 different options to customize your accounting page to your liking. Each one is useful for your transactions as it fits your needs.

It is recommended to sync your bank account; this will automatically upload your online banking records. No automatic uploading is needed making this the most convenient option for our users. Waves uses state of the art encryption to make sure sensitive information is secure.

The other options are to upload a CSV statement transaction. This is done by uploading a bank statement from your computer. The user can also manually enter transaction amounts as either an income or expense.
